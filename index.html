Komouri Meaning

Komouri is Japanese for "bat". He had large ears when he was a baby, and resembled a baby bat. Today, however, he resembles more of a Puma!

Komouri was originally part of a litter of five kittens. My sisters had name the other four, and he happened to be the one I had named because he looked like a little bat when he arrived. Initially, they had been brought to us at about two weeks old in a flea infested cardboard box. One year later, he has grown tremendously, and lives in a house full of love. He has two older sisters named Goldie and MeowMeow.
